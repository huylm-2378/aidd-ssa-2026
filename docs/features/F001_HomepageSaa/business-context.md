## Why It Matters

Sun* Annual Awards 2025 (theme "Root Further") is Sun*'s yearly recognition event, and this
homepage is the single public landing page that builds anticipation for it — showing when and
where the event happens, what awards are up for grabs, and introducing the new "Sun* Kudos"
peer-recognition program launching alongside it. It needs to work for any visitor, without
requiring anyone to log in first.

## Who Uses It

- **Sunners (employees)** — visit to check the countdown to the event, look up award categories,
  and learn about Sun* Kudos before it launches.
- **Event organizers / BTC** — share this page as the public source of truth for event date,
  venue, and award information ahead of the ceremony.
- **Prospective/general visitors** — anyone following the Sun* Annual Awards event who lands on
  this page from a shared link, with no account needed.

## What They Do

1. A visitor opens the homepage and sees the event countdown, date, venue, and broadcast details
   at a glance.
2. The visitor browses the six award categories, reading a short description of each.
3. The visitor selects an award category to view its full details on a dedicated award-information
   page.
4. The visitor reads about the new Sun* Kudos recognition program and can jump to a page with more
   details.
5. The visitor can switch the page's language between Vietnamese and English at any time.
